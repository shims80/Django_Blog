About :

I love Vacation blog was created as final project for my full stack development bootcamp.

For me traveling is a way of life and the purest expression for the word "freedom" as i was lucky to travel to many beautiful countries around the world meet amazing people and to record unforgettable memories in my mind. This blog came out as a good opportunity to bring all this new and old memories out and with hope that it will be useful to others.

How to use this blog :

I love vacation unlike other blog is a personal and public blog.it means that anyone can create a profile by registering to this blog website And post their traveling experience vacation review or recommendations.

In the new post content section you can shear a post (please mention in the title the name of the place and the name of the country in parentheses) e.g. Heaven on earth - Maui (Hawaii) you can add multiply linked images to the post as you like using the rich text editor or a single image at the end of the post content with the add image button.for each post there's a start ratings widget and a comments section to express feeling or thoughts about the post. if you like to ask a general equation or to chat with other traveling mates you can use our chat room. (Must be a registered user)

In the profile page a user can update his profile image and if you like to search the blog just enter a related search word in the search bar anywhere in the blog press enter and all posts contening the related searched word will be displayed.

*Other questions regarding the use of this blog or general questions please use the feedback widget in the home page
